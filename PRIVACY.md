# Privacy Policy for Canvas App

**Last Updated:** December 4, 2025

## Introduction
Canvas ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

## Data We Collect
We use Apple's **SensorKit** framework to collect the following data types, strictly for the purpose of understanding your daily activities and digital wellbeing:

1.  **Visits (Location)**:
    -   We collect data about significant locations you visit (e.g., Home, Work) and your arrival/departure times.
    -   **Purpose**: To correlate your activity patterns with your environment.

2.  **Device Usage**:
    -   We collect metrics on how you use your device, including screen wakes, unlocks, and total screen time.
    -   **Purpose**: To analyze your digital wellbeing and device usage habits.

## How We Use Your Data
-   **On-Device Processing**: All data analysis and model training occur locally on your device. We do not upload your raw sensor data to any external servers.
-   **Insights**: We use the processed data to provide you with personalized insights about your daily routine and "Vibe" status.

## Data Sharing
-   We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties.
-   Data is only stored locally on your device and is managed by the operating system's secure storage.

## Your Rights and Control
-   **Authorization**: You have full control over which sensors we can access. You can grant or revoke permission for "Visits" or "Device Usage" at any time in your device Settings > Privacy & Security > Research Sensor & Usage Data.
-   **Data Deletion**: You can delete your collected data at any time through the application settings or by deleting the app.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
